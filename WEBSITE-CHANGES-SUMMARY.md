# Gambino Gold Website Changes Summary

**Date:** November 25, 2025
**Purpose:** Regulatory compliance positioning and legal consultation preparation

---

## 1. Overview

The gambino.gold website has undergone significant updates to position Gambino Gold as a **software infrastructure provider** rather than a gambling or gaming platform. These changes prepare the company for legal consultation regarding cryptocurrency mining operations and regulatory compliance.

Key objectives:
- Establish clear positioning as technology/infrastructure licensing company
- Remove any gambling-adjacent language or imagery
- Add appropriate compliance disclaimers and age restrictions
- Prepare documentation for regulatory review

## 7. Compliance Positioning

### Key Disclaimers Now in Place

1. **Age Restriction**
   > "18+ only at licensed partner venues"

2. **Infrastructure Provider Clarification**
   > "Gambino Gold provides software infrastructure only—all money transmission handled by licensed financial entities"

3. **Utility Token Clarification**
   > "Utility tokens enable governance and network access—not investment products. Not gambling or securities."

4. **Development Status**
   > "Values shown are illustrative examples only. Actual tier requirements and benefits are under development and subject to change."

5. **Tennessee Pilot Disclosure**
   > Whitepaper includes disclosure of current pilot program in Tennessee sweepstakes gaming venues

### Regulatory Language Added

- "Software Infrastructure Provider" positioning throughout
- "Licensed Implementation Partners" terminology for B2B model
- "Compliant blockchain technology" messaging
- "Transparent, traceable, and taxable" framework language
- References to AML policy and regulatory compliance pages
- "Regulated Entity" indicator in footer
